Schedule

Here's the schedule for our meetings this Semester

Spring 2024

02/08 - Introduction Meeting, 6:00PM Morrill II 222

This will be our first meeting in the Spring '24 semester. There will be an introduction to the Board members, and we will talk about all of the plans the club has for the semester!

02/29 - Undergrad Reasearch Panel, 6:00 PM ILC S131

Meeting where we will host 4 Undergrad Researchers who will share their experiences to the club, Pizza provided as always!

03/13 - Alumni Night, 7:00PM ILC S240

This will be our third meeting, where BURC will host an alumni panel where they will discuss their experiences while at school and beyond. Bring your friends and join us over FREE PIZZA!

04/11 - BUA Application Workshop, 6:45 ILC S220

This will be our fourth meeting, where we'll host two BUA applicants, work on Applications with members, live resume building, and have a Linkedin workshop.

05/02 - Research Presentations #1

This will be our fifth meeting for the semester, we'll host seniors presenting their research before their thesis defenses.

05/09 - Research Presentations #2

This will be our final meeting for the Spring '24 Semester, a continuation of our classic event where we host undergraduate students presenting their research to the audience.
