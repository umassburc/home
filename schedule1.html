Schedule

Here's the schedule for our meetings this Semester

Spring 2024

02/08 - Introduction Meeting

This will be our first meeting in the Spring '24 semester. There will be an introduction to the Board members, and we will talk about all of the plans the club has for the semester!

02/29 6:00 PM ILC S131 - Undergrad Reasearch Panel

Meeting where we will host 4 Undergrad Researchers who will share their experiences to the club, Pizza provided as always!

03/13 - Alumni Night

This will be our third meeting, where BURC will host an alumni panel where they will discuss their experiences while at school and beyond.

04/04 - PI Panel

This will be our fourth meeting, where guest Principal Investigators will share their experience and talk about the world of research at UMass.

04/18 - BUA Application Cycle Workshop

Meeting where BURC will host a workshop to prepare students for the upcoming BUA application cycle.

05/02 - Research Presentations

This will be our final meeting for the Spring '24 Semester, our classic event where we host undergraduate students where they present their research to the audience.
