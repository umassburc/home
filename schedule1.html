Schedule

Here's the schedule for our meetings this Semester

Fall 2024

Week of 10/28 Meeting

More information to come, stay tuned to our newsletter to be up to date with BURC for upcoming meeting.

10/16 - B.U.R.C. x BioSci, 5:30PM ILC S120

Come join us for a symposium style event with the BioSci club! Join us and engage in conversation with fellow undergraduate researchers, learn about what they do, and see what interests you!

09/30 - Undergrad Researcher Panel, 7:00PM ILC S140

Join us for an undergraduate research panel. Make sure to come prepared with any questions, comments, or concerns you have about research for your fellow undergraduates.

09/19 - Introduction Meeting, 7:00PM ILC S140

This will be our first meeting in the Fall '24 semester. There will be an introduction to the Board members, and we will introduce BURC and what to expect from the club this academic year.

Future Meetings

Stay tuned for the next BURC meetings this Fall, we will update the website with more information as soon as it becomes available.

Feedback Form

Spring 2024

04/11 - BUA Application Workshop, 6:45 ILC S220

This will be our fourth meeting, where we'll host two BUA applicants, work on Applications with members, live resume building, and have a Linkedin workshop.

05/02 - Research Presentations #1

This will be our fifth meeting for the semester, we'll host seniors presenting their research before their thesis defenses.

05/09 - Research Presentations #2

This will be our final meeting for the Spring '24 Semester, a continuation of our classic event where we host undergraduate students presenting their research to the audience.
